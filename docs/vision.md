# OpenMidway — Vision

## What We Are Building

OpenMidway is a user-friendly original Xbox emulator focused on multiplayer and
Windows usability.

> "The easiest way to play original Xbox games online and locally — with modern
> performance, stability, and multiplayer tools."

---

## Core Pillars

1. **Multiplayer-first emulator** — System Link, tunneling, real hardware
2. **Windows-native experience** — auto-detection, no janky setup
3. **Performance + stability over perfection** — good enough to play, today
4. **Gradual accuracy improvements** — GPU and memory improvements over time

---

## Strategic Approach

We win by:
- Making multiplayer easy
- Making setup painless
- Making performance good enough

Then we improve accuracy over time.

We do **not** fall into the trap of "let's perfectly emulate the Xbox first."
People use emulators because they want to play games, not because they want
perfect cycle accuracy.

---

## Target Audience

- Windows users who want to play original Xbox games with friends online
- Players who want a five-minute setup experience
- Anyone frustrated by the complexity of existing emulator networking setups

---

## Legal Note

OpenMidway does **not** aim to re-implement or bypass native Xbox Live
(Microsoft's proprietary online service). System Link / LAN tunneling operates
entirely at the network layer without touching Xbox Live authentication, title
licensing, or Microsoft's servers.
